Hearty Winter Pork Stew

Recipe by Rural Roots Meats

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs boneless pork roast, cut into 1" cubes
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoon salt, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoon black pepper, divided
  • 3 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 5 carrots, peeled and cut into chunks
  • 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cut into 1" chunks
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Instructions:

  1. **Prep the Pork:** In a bowl, combine the flour, 1 tsp salt, and 1 tsp pepper. Dredge the pork cubes in the flour mixture.
  2. **Brown the Pork:** In a large Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Brown the pork in batches and set aside.
  3. **Sauté Aromatics:** Add the onion to the pot and sauté until softened. Add the garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. **Simmer the Stew:** Return the pork to the pot. Add carrots, potatoes, chicken broth, diced tomatoes, bay leaves, and the remaining salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 1.5 - 2 hours, or until pork is tender.
  5. **Finish and Serve:** Stir in the frozen peas and cook for another 5 minutes. Remove bay leaves before serving.
