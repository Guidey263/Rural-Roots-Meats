Sweet & Savory Grilled Pork Skewers

Recipe by Rural Roots Meats

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs pork tenderloin, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 large bell pepper (any color), cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 red onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 8-12 wooden or metal skewers (if using wooden, soak in water for 30 minutes)
For the Marinade/Glaze:
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup honey or maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. **Marinate Pork:** In a medium bowl, whisk together soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, ginger, garlic, and sesame oil. Reserve about ¼ cup of this marinade for basting later. Add the pork cubes to the remaining marinade, toss to coat, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. **Prepare Skewers:** Thread the marinated pork cubes, bell pepper pieces, and red onion pieces onto the skewers, alternating them.
  3. **Preheat Grill:** Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Lightly oil the grill grates.
  4. **Grill Skewers:** Place the skewers on the hot grill. Grill for 8-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the pork is cooked through and lightly charred. The internal temperature of the pork should reach 145°F (63°C).
  5. **Baste & Serve:** During the last few minutes of grilling, brush the skewers with the reserved marinade. Serve immediately.
