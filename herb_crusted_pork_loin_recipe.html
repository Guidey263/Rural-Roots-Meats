Slow Roasted Herb Crusted Pork Loin

Recipe by Rural Roots Meats

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 lb boneless pork loin roast
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth (for the pan)

Instructions:

  1. **Prep Pork & Preheat Oven:** Pat the pork loin dry. Preheat oven to 275째F (135째C).
  2. **Make Herb Paste:** In a small bowl, combine olive oil, rosemary, thyme, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, salt, and black pepper.
  3. **Coat Pork Loin:** Rub the herb paste all over the pork loin.
  4. **Prepare Roasting Pan:** Place the pork loin on a rack in a roasting pan. Pour the chicken broth into the bottom of the pan.
  5. **Slow Roast:** Roast for 2-3 hours, or until an internal thermometer registers 145째F (63째C).
  6. **Rest & Serve:** Remove the pork loin from the oven, tent it with foil, and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
