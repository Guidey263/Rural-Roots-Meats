Meet the Family Behind Rural Roots Meats

Published: September 16, 2025

Welcome! We're the family behind Rural Roots Meats, and we're thrilled to share a little piece of our world with you. Our farm isn't just a business; it's our home, our passion, and our commitment to a way of life that we believe in deeply.

From a Dream to a Dinner Table

Rural Roots Meats started from a simple desire: to feed our own family with wholesome, ethically-raised food. We wanted to know exactly where our food came from and how it was raised. This journey quickly grew into a passion for sustainable agriculture and providing that same peace of mind to our friends, neighbors, and the wider community here in Murehwa.
