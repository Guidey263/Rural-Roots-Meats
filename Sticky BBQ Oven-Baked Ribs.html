Sticky BBQ Oven Baked Ribs

Recipe by Rural Roots Meats

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 racks of pork ribs
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1.5 cups your favorite BBQ sauce

Instructions:

  1. **Preheat Oven and Prep Ribs:** Preheat your oven to 300°F (150°C). Remove the thin membrane from the back of the ribs. Pat them thoroughly dry.
  2. **Make the Rub:** In a small bowl, combine paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and salt.
  3. **Season the Ribs:** Generously rub the spice mixture all over both sides of the ribs.
  4. **Wrap the Ribs:** Wrap each rack of ribs tightly in heavy-duty aluminum foil. Place on a baking sheet.
  5. **Bake the Ribs:** Bake for 2 to 2.5 hours, or until the meat is tender.
  6. **Apply BBQ Sauce:** Carefully unwrap the ribs. Brush BBQ sauce generously over both sides.
  7. **Glaze and Broil/Bake:** Return the ribs to the oven, uncovered, for another 20-30 minutes, or until the sauce is bubbly. For an extra sticky glaze, broil for the last 2-3 minutes, watching closely.
  8. **Rest and Serve:** Let the ribs rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
